Features

Why people use Lumi

No fluff. Just the stuff that matters.

⚡

Instant downloads

No countdowns, no rate limits. Lumi pulls at your full connection speed the moment you hit paste.

🎵

Audio extraction

Strip the video, keep the sound. Clean MP3 or FLAC with metadata and album art intact.

📋

Batch playlists

Paste a playlist link, walk away. Lumi queues every track and downloads them all.

🔒

Fully offline

No account, no cloud, no tracking. Files go straight to your folder and stay there.

How it works

Three steps

Zero setup. Open the app and go.

1

Paste a URL

2

Pick your format

4K, 1080p, MP3, FLAC, subtitles — pick exactly what you want.

3

It's in your folder

Saved to your chosen folder. No browser tray, no temp files.
